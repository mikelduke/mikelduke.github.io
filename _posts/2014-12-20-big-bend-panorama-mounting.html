I mounted a panorama I created last year at Big Bend at the Dallas Makerspace last night. I printed it on paper as 12.4" x 36" with 2" borders. I then built a simple frame and wrapped the photo like it was canvas. I've tried a few in this way, and it looks good while being inexpensive.
